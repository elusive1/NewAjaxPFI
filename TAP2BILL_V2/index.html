Direct Carrier Billing

Direct carrier billing (DCB) is an online mobile payment method which allows users to make purchases by charging payments to their mobile phone carrier bill.

Premium SMS Billing

SMS Billing is an easy and simple to use payment mechanism, often used for charity donations, TV/radio votes and competitions providing instant billing in real time.
